In the Design World

Black Peony Interiors among the most celebrated names in interior design — from prestigious show houses to exclusive gallery events.

Kips Bay Decorator Show House

New York

One of the most prestigious design events in the country, the Kips Bay Decorator Show House has inspired and challenged designers since its founding in 1973. Top design talents are invited to transform distinguished residential spaces, creating inspiring rooms that showcase their vision and craft. Through decades of celebrated exhibitions, the show house has raised substantial support for the Kips Bay Boys & Girls Club.

Kips Bay Decorator Show House

Dallas

The Dallas edition brings the same caliber of design excellence to one of the most vibrant design communities in the South. Salone del Mobile Milano

Milan, Italy

The global benchmark for furniture and design since 1961, Salone del Mobile Milano is the world's most influential design fair. Each year, over 1,900 exhibitors from more than 30 countries gather across Milan to present the very best in furnishing, lighting, and material innovation. It is the event that defines the future of contemporary design — and a destination Black Peony is proud to attend.

Texas Design Week

Houston, Texas · April 2026

Texas Design Week Houston 2026 (April 13–16) unfolded across the city's leading design addresses, including Decorative Center Houston, Perigold, Park House, Hästens Houston, and Window Shop by Japhet Bates Collective. Black Peony Interiors was present for the book signing of Gene Pressman, engaging with the week's editorial dialogue and design culture.
